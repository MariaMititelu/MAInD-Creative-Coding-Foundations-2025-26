  • The book thief by Markus Zusak

    A very emotional, deep and heart-breaking book! This is by far one of my favourite books. I really enjoyed reading it. I was hooked by Zusak idea of narator's point of view.

  • Jezebel by Irene Nemirovsky

    This book both shocked me and hooked me. I was also so angry with the main character!!!! This book really made me be glad about the relationship that I have with my mom! Anyway, this book is a 10!

  • Ariadne by Jennifer Saint

    I absolutely love mythology books, but I ADORE the ones that retell the myth from a feminine perspective!

  • The fifth wave by Rick Yancey

    This is by far my favourite SF trilogy. Absolutely breath-taking and engaging! I remember that I literally devoured the whole 3 books!

  • Almond by Won-Pyung Sohn

    One of my favourite books. I literally devoured it in one sit! I absolutely loved how the charater changed from the beggining to the end.

  • The girl with the louding voice by Abe Dare

    I am a newby when it comes to African literature, but this book just made me fall in love with this kind of books! I can't wait to read the continuation of this book!
